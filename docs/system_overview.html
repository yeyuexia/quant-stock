What the system does, in one breath系统功能一句话概括

Every trading day the system asks three questions and answers each with the right tool:

每个交易日，系统会提出三个问题，并用合适的工具各自作答：

What should I buy?我应该买什么？

Two independent strategies each rank stocks (one on cheapness+quality, one on growth+momentum). A single AI agent reads both lists and picks the top 4. Slow, judgment-heavy → runs once a day.

两套独立策略各自对股票排名（一套基于低估+质量，一套基于成长+动量）。一个AI 智能体综合两份榜单，选出前 4 名。决策慢而重、需要判断力 → 每日运行一次。

When do I buy it?我应该什么时候买？

The watchdog watches those 4 names intraday and fires a buy only when a coded volume-breakout rule confirms the move. Fast, deterministic → runs every 5 minutes.

watchdog 在盘中持续监控这 4 只标的，仅当量能突破的编码规则确认信号时才触发买入。快速、确定性 → 每 5 分钟运行一次。

How do I not blow up?如何避免爆仓？

Every order passes a safety layer (kill-switch, cash check, daily caps, large-order gate). Positions get stop-losses and rule-based exits. Nothing trades on real money — it's a paper account.

每笔订单都经过安全层审核（熔断开关、现金校验、每日限额、大单审批）。所有持仓配备止损和规则驱动的出场机制。系统仅操作模拟账户，不涉及真实资金。

Design principle that runs through everything: separate the slow, smart, daily decision (which stocks — done by the LLM) from the fast, deterministic, high-frequency decision (when to buy / when to exit — done by code). The LLM never sits on the intraday hot loop.

贯穿全系统的设计原则：将慢速、智能、每日一次的决策（选哪些股票——由 LLM 完成）与快速、确定性、高频的决策（何时买入 / 何时出场——由代码完成）严格分离。LLM 永远不会出现在盘中热循环中。

Buy candidates买入候选

.cache/buy_candidates.json — the only names the watchdog will consider buying that day. Read-only to the watchdog; a staleness guard ignores it if the daily job hasn't refreshed it.当日 watchdog 唯一会考虑买入的标的列表。对 watchdog 为只读；若每日任务未刷新，过期保护机制将忽略该文件。

Why it runs once a day, not every 5 minutes: the agent's only inputs are the daily screens — re-running the LLM intraday can't produce a better answer (no new information), just more cost and churn. So selection happens once, in the pre-market watchdog run; the intraday loop only does coded timing.

为何每日只运行一次而非每 5 分钟：智能体的唯一输入是每日筛选结果——盘中重复调用 LLM 不会产生更好的答案（没有新信息），只会增加成本和扰动。因此，选股只在盘前 watchdog 运行时执行一次；盘中循环仅负责编码择时。

screener.pysignals信号

CANSLIM technical screen: quarterly EPS/revenue growth pre-filter, relative-strength percentile, and VCP (Volatility Contraction Pattern) base detection with a buy pivot. One of the two ensemble strategies.

CANSLIM 技术筛选：季度 EPS/营收增长预过滤、相对强度百分位，以及带买入枢轴的 VCP（波动率收缩形态）形态识别。集成策略之一。

value_screen.pysignals信号

Two-track value screen over the Russell 3000 (iShares IWV CSV). A stage-0 price/volume pre-filter cuts ~3000 → a few hundred (no fundamentals calls); survivors' fundamentals are fetched in parallel and split into Track A (profitable "mispriced bargains" — PEG/PE cheap + growth + solvency) and Track B (unprofitable growth — P/S cheap + high revenue growth + cash-runway), then ranked and interleaved. Thresholds live in config.VS_TRACK_A/VS_TRACK_B. The "low value, high potential" source.

针对 Russell 3000（iShares IWV CSV）的双轨价值筛选。第 0 阶段价格/成交量预过滤将约 3000 只股票压缩至数百只（不调用基本面接口）；幸存者的基本面数据并行获取，分为 Track A（盈利的"价值洼地"——PEG/PE 低估 + 成长 + 偿债能力）和 Track B（不盈利的成长股——P/S 低估 + 营收高增长 + 现金储备），排名后交错合并。阈值存储于 config.VS_TRACK_A/VS_TRACK_B。这是"低估值、高潜力"的来源。

momentum.py / macro.pysignals信号

Dual-momentum ETF rotation for the core sleeve, and a FRED-based macro regime overlay that shrinks risk in hostile regimes and can trigger macro-driven exits.

针对核心份额的双动量 ETF 轮动，以及基于 FRED 数据的宏观环境覆盖层——在不利环境下收缩风险敞口，并可触发宏观驱动的出场。

quant/news/ (political forecast)signals信号

Political-forecast subsystem: RSS political-event classification → SQLite news store → LLM hotspot/briefing analysis. Blends a political-risk score (30%) into the macro equity adjustment and surfaces a watchdog POLITICAL alert; the Telegram bot's /forecast + /hotspots read from it.

政治预测子系统：RSS 政治事件分类 → SQLite 新闻库 → LLM 热点/简报分析。将政治风险分数（30%）融入宏观股票仓位调整，并在看门狗中产生 POLITICAL 告警；Telegram 机器人的 /forecast 与 /hotspots 从中读取数据。

strategies.pyselection选股

The isolated-strategy contract + parallel runner. Each strategy writes {ticker, score, rank, factors} rows to its own JSON file; a strategy that errors or hangs is skipped (bounded by a timeout) so one can never stall the others or the watchdog.

隔离策略契约 + 并行运行器。每套策略将 {ticker, score, rank, factors} 行写入各自的 JSON 文件；出错或挂起的策略会被跳过（受超时限制），确保单套策略不会阻塞其他策略或 watchdog。

investor_agent.pyselection选股

Five-stage LLM due-diligence pipeline. select_candidates(): (1) merges strategy pools, excludes owned names; (2) builds peer-relative dossiers in parallel (price/PE/target, EPS surprises, news, sector z-scores); (3) produces a balanced blind shortlist (source-cap prevents one strategy dominating, labels stripped to remove positivity bias, source mix logged to .cache/agent_source_mix.csv); (4) Analyst stage writes structured bull/bear verdicts, Critic stage prunes weak ones; (5) PM stage selects 0–AGENT_MAX_PICKS final picks with advisory buy_low/buy_high/stop_loss/take_profit levels (may abstain; picks below AGENT_CONVICTION_FLOOR are dropped). Rationale and levels appended to .cache/agent_reasons.log. Falls back to rule-ranking on any LLM failure.

五阶段 LLM 尽职调查流水线。select_candidates()：(1) 合并各策略候选池，排除已持有标的；(2) 并行构建同行相对档案（价格/PE/目标价、EPS 超预期、新闻、行业 z-score）；(3) 生成均衡盲选短名单（来源上限防止单策略主导，去除标签以消除正向偏差，来源混合比例记录至 .cache/agent_source_mix.csv）；(4) Analyst 阶段撰写结构化多空判断，Critic 阶段剔除弱势标的；(5) PM 阶段选出 0–AGENT_MAX_PICKS 个最终标的，附带建议的 buy_low/buy_high/stop_loss/take_profit 水平（可能弃权；低于 AGENT_CONVICTION_FLOOR 的标的被剔除）。理由和水平追加至 .cache/agent_reasons.log。任何 LLM 失败均回退到规则排名。

watchdog.pytiming择时

The daily + intraday monitor. Daily run: sync, stop attach, macro, and the once-a-day ensemble trigger. Intraday run (every 5 min, RTH): coded buy signals from the candidate list, stop-loss enforcement (auto market-sell on breach), and SEPA exits. The candidate source prefers buy_candidates.json and falls back to the raw screener.

每日 + 盘中监控器。每日运行：同步、补充止损、宏观检查，以及每日一次的集成触发。盘中运行（每 5 分钟，正常交易时段）：来自候选列表的编码买入信号、止损执行（触发时自动市价卖出）以及 SEPA 出场。候选来源优先使用 buy_candidates.json，回退至原始筛选器。

sepa_exits.pytiming择时

Pure Minervini SEPA exit rules: R-multiple scale-outs, failed-breakout detection, climax-top detection, and a 21-EMA trailing backstop. The watchdog calls into it.

纯 Minervini SEPA 出场规则：R 倍数分批平仓、突破失败检测、顶部高潮形态检测，以及 21-EMA 追踪止损。由 watchdog 调用。

rebalancer.pyexecution执行

Builds target weights per tranche from the signals, sends the tiny actionable slice through quant/execution/orders.py, and writes the rest to pending_plan.json for the intraday executor.

根据信号为每个份额构建目标权重，将可立即执行的小额部分通过 quant/execution/orders.py 提交，其余写入 pending_plan.json 供盘中 executor 处理。

executor.pyexecution执行

Intraday driver (every 10 min, RTH). Consumes the pending plan, evaluates 5 circuit breakers (SPY drop, VIX spike, single-name shock, news shock, macro flip), and submits sliced marketable-limit orders.

盘中驱动器（每 10 分钟，正常交易时段）。消费待执行计划，评估 5 个熔断器（SPY 下跌、VIX 飙升、个股冲击、新闻冲击、宏观反转），并提交分批可成交限价单。

planner.py / planning.pyexecution执行

Pure functions that enrich raw order intents with execution tier, price limits, and slice counts. No I/O.

纯函数，为原始订单意图补充执行层级、价格限制和分批数量。无 I/O。

orders.pypolicy策略

The policy chokepoint. sync_state reconciles live positions and adopts broker-imported holdings into a sleeve; execute_plan applies all safety rails; pending-queue + stop helpers live here. Every order goes through it.

策略核心节点。sync_state 核对实时持仓并将经纪商导入的持仓纳入份额管理；execute_plan 执行所有安全规则；待执行队列和止损辅助函数均在此处。所有订单必经此模块。

broker.pydata数据

Thin Alpaca SDK wrapper — pure I/O, raises BrokerError, leaks no SDK objects. The paper/live guard requires two explicit env flags to ever touch a live account.

精简的 Alpaca SDK 封装层——纯 I/O，抛出 BrokerError，不暴露任何 SDK 对象。模拟/实盘防护要求同时设置两个明确的环境变量才能接触实盘账户。

Why it doesn't blow up为何不会爆仓

Risk & safety rails风险与安全护栏

Every order — no matter the trigger — passes these in quant/execution/orders.py:

每笔订单——无论何种触发方式——都会经过 quant/execution/orders.py 中的以下检查：

HALT kill-switchHALT 熔断开关

Drop a .cache/HALT file and all order logic exits cleanly. touch to pause, rm to resume.

创建 .cache/HALT 文件即可干净地终止所有订单逻辑。touch 暂停，rm 恢复。

Paper / live guard模拟/实盘防护

Live trading needs both ALPACA_ENV=live and an explicit confirm flag. Any single-flag typo stays on paper.

实盘交易需要同时设置 ALPACA_ENV=live 和明确的确认标志。任何单标志误操作均停留在模拟模式。

Cash-aware gate资金感知门控

Buys can't exceed available cash (unless margin is explicitly allowed); a broker-fetch failure fails closed.

买入不得超过可用现金（除非明确允许使用保证金）；经纪商数据获取失败时默认拒绝。

Daily caps每日限额

Hard limits on order count and total notional per day; excess defers to tomorrow.

每日订单数量和总名义金额的硬性上限；超额部分推迟至次日。

Large-order approval大单审批

Orders above a threshold queue to pending_orders.json for Telegram approval instead of firing.

超过阈值的订单进入 pending_orders.json 队列，等待 Telegram 审批，而非直接执行。

Position adoption + starvation alert持仓纳管 + 资源匮乏告警

Broker-imported "unknown" positions are auto-tagged into a sleeve so the rebalancer manages them; a loud alert fires if untagged value would starve it.

经纪商导入的"未知"持仓自动标记纳入某份额，由再平衡器统一管理；若未标记的仓值会导致份额资金匮乏，则触发显著告警。

Fractional-safe stops碎股安全止损

Native stops can't attach to fractional shares, so the watchdog enforces stops by market-selling on breach — anchored to each position's adoption date so a freshly-adopted name isn't liquidated against an old high.

原生止损单不支持碎股，因此 watchdog 通过触发时市价卖出来执行止损——锚定每个持仓的纳管日期，避免新纳管标的被旧高点触发清仓。

Candidate staleness guard候选过期保护

If the daily ensemble job hasn't refreshed buy_candidates.json within a max age, the watchdog ignores it and falls back — so auto-buys never run on stale picks.

若每日集成任务未在最大时限内刷新 buy_candidates.json，watchdog 将忽略该文件并回退——确保自动买入不会基于过期标的执行。

Getting out出场机制

Exit logic出场逻辑

Selling is rule-driven, never discretionary. The core stock sleeve uses Minervini SEPA; everything has a stop.

卖出由规则驱动，从不依赖主观判断。核心股票份额使用 Minervini SEPA；所有持仓均设有止损。

Stop-loss止损

Per-sleeve stop (ATR-scaled for core, fixed for aggressive). Breach → market sell, enforced intraday.

按份额设置止损（核心份额以 ATR 为比例，激进份额固定比例）。触发 → 市价卖出，盘中执行。

Trailing stop追踪止损

Peak-to-current trail, measured from when the position was first managed (not its full history).

从峰值到当前的追踪止损，从持仓首次纳管时开始计量（而非完整历史）。

SEPA R-multiple scale-outSEPA R 倍数分批平仓

Sell a fraction at 2R and 3R profit tiers, banking gains as the move extends.

在 2R 和 3R 盈利节点分批卖出，随行情延伸逐步锁定收益。

Failed breakout突破失败

If a fresh breakout reverses below its pivot within a few days, exit — the thesis failed.

若新突破在数日内回落至枢轴下方，立即出场——逻辑已证伪。

Climax top顶部高潮

Parabolic blow-off detection (extended run + range/volume expansion) tightens the trail.

抛物线式高潮顶部检测（持续上涨 + 振幅/成交量放大）收紧追踪止损。

21-EMA backstop托底止损

After the final scale-out tier, exit the remainder on a daily close below the 21-EMA.

最后一批分批平仓后，若日收盘价跌破 21-EMA，则清仓剩余仓位。

Macro flip宏观反转

A hostile macro-regime change can drive defensive exits the next day.

Glossary术语表

Tranche
A sub-portfolio with its own rules. Core (~90%) = diversified ETFs + screened stocks; Aggressive (~10%) = leveraged ETF momentum.具有独立规则的子投资组合。核心（约 90%）= 多元化 ETF + 筛选股票；激进（约 10%）= 杠杆 ETF 动量。
CANSLIM / VCP
A growth-stock methodology (O'Neil) and the tight "base" pattern that precedes a breakout — the buy pivot.欧尼尔的成长股方法论，以及突破前的紧密"底部"形态——买入枢轴。
SEPA
Minervini's Specific Entry Point Analysis — the rule set for scaling out of winners and cutting failures.Minervini 的特定入场点分析——用于分批平仓盈利和及时止损亏损的规则集。
R-multiple
Profit measured in units of initial risk. "2R" = twice the per-share risk taken at entry.以初始风险单位衡量的盈利。"2R" = 入场时每股风险的两倍。
Ensemble
Running multiple independent strategies and combining their outputs — here, via the AI agent's top-N pick.运行多套独立策略并合并其输出——此处通过 AI 智能体的前 N 个选股实现。
Buy candidates买入候选
The agent's daily shortlist (top 4). The only names the watchdog will consider buying that day.智能体每日短名单（前 4 名）。当日 watchdog 唯一会考虑买入的标的。
